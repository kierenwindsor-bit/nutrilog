💬 Help

How to use NutriLog with Claude

Logging meals with Claude AI

1
Open Claude.ai and go to your NutriLog chat conversation
2
Tell Claude what you ate — "had a chicken wrap and a cappuccino for lunch"
3
Claude calculates kcal & protein and gives you the numbers
4
Come back here, tap + on the right meal slot, and enter the numbers

📲 Installing as a phone app

iPhone: Open this page in Safari → tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"

Android: Open in Chrome → tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home Screen"

🔄 Updating the app

Via Netlify Drop (easiest):
1. Ask Claude for the updated HTML file
2. Go to app.netlify.com → log in → click your site
3. Click the Deploys tab
4. Drag & drop the new HTML file into the deploy box
5. Done — same URL, instant update ✓

Via GitHub Pages:
1. Go to your repo on github.com
2. Click the file → pencil ✏️ icon
3. Select all, paste the new code
4. Click Commit changes — updates in ~60 seconds ✓

📅 Weekly Check-in — Every Monday

To track how fast you're losing weight and keep your calorie target dialled in, message Claude every Monday morning with:

1. Your weight that morning (kg)
2. How the week felt — hungry? tired? training OK?
3. Your average daily calories from the Week tab

Claude will compare your actual weight loss to your expected loss and adjust your daily calorie target if needed.

Current target: 2,100 kcal/day = −0.5 kg/week

Lost less than 0.3kg → drop to ~1,900 kcal
Lost more than 0.7kg → increase to ~2,200 kcal
Lost ~0.5kg → stay the course ✓

⚠️ About your data

Your logged meals are saved to this device's local storage. They persist between visits but are tied to this browser. If you clear your browser data or switch devices, you'll start fresh. Your data is never sent anywhere.
